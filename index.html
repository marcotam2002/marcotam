About Me

I am a master student at Institute of Science Tokyoe, advised by Prof. Rei Kawakami. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics from The Chinese University of Hong Kong. My research interests include Computational Imaging, 3D Vision and Generative AI. Specifically, I am currently focusing on topics such as pointmap estimations and generative computational imaging, etc.
